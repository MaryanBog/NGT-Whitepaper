# NGT-2.0 — Grant Proposal
### Structural Reserve Protocol Based on Flexion Dynamics V2.0

## 1. Project Summary
NGT-2.0 is a next-generation economic protocol that maintains long-term structural viability through formal mathematical dynamics, not market signals or heuristics.  
The system uses the structural state:

X = (Δ, Φ, M, κ)

to ensure it always stays inside the Viability Domain D, where structural reversibility and stability are guaranteed.

NGT-2.0 introduces the world’s first **Structural Reserve Protocol** — an economic organism that manages deviation, energy, memory, and contractivity to preserve its structural life indefinitely.

---

## 2. Problem
Existing economic protocols — stablecoins, algorithmic systems, indices, treasuries, DAOs — all suffer from structural decay:

- irreversible damage accumulates  
- liquidity symmetry degrades  
- reserve operations become dangerous  
- corrections amplify collapse  
- no geometry of viability exists  

NGT 1.x had the same limitations due to:

- one-dimensional deviation  
- no memory/friction tracking  
- no collapse boundary  
- no contractivity geometry  
- no viability region  

These weaknesses make current systems fragile and unable to survive extreme conditions.

---

## 3. Solution: NGT-2.0
NGT-2.0 solves structural decay by introducing a mathematically defined structural space where the system remains alive.

Core innovations:

- **Structural Flow** instead of price reactivity  
- **Viability Domain** (Φ, M, Δ, κ)  
- **Collapse Boundary** (irreversible failure surface)  
- **Vault as reversibility buffer**  
- **Reserve as structural substrate**  
- **Governance 2.0** (humans set boundaries, system acts autonomously)  
- **Contractivity Safety Engine (κ ≥ 0)**  

No heuristics.  
No liquidation cascades.  
No speculative stabilization logic.

A deterministic structural organism replaces traditional economic design.
